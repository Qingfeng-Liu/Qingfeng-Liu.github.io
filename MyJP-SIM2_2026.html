MyJP-SIM²

Meeting You in JAPAN - Symposium on Innovative Mathematical Minds

March 26–29, 2026
Hosei University, Koganei Campus, Tokyo, Japan

MyJP-SIM² is an international symposium organized under the Transdisciplinary Econometrics & Data Science Seminar (TEDS), providing a compact forum for creative ideas in statistics, econometrics, data science, machine learning, and related fields.

No registration fee · Partner hotel with discounted rate · Self-funded dinner gathering

About the Symposium

MyJP-SIM²: Symposium on Innovative Mathematical Minds aims to bring together researchers working at the frontier of mathematical statistics, econometrics, and data science. The meeting is intentionally compact, encouraging ample discussion time, informal exchanges, and the exploration of new ideas across disciplines.

The symposium is held at Hosei University’s Koganei Campus in Tokyo, Japan. A partner hotel will be designated for participants, and a self-funded dinner gathering will be organized to promote networking in a relaxed setting.

Topics of Interest

We welcome research contributions across a broad range of areas, including but not limited to:

  • Modern statistical theory and methodology
  • Machine learning, deep learning, and representation learning
  • Generative AI, foundation models, and multimodal learning
  • Causal inference, reinforcement learning, and decision intelligence
  • High-dimensional statistics, sparsity, and nonparametric modeling
  • Econometrics, financial econometrics, and computational economics
  • Network analysis, graph learning, and complex systems
  • Federated learning, privacy-preserving modeling, and robust/secure AI
  • Applications in economics, finance, policy analysis, and the social sciences
  • Transdisciplinary approaches linking data science, mathematics, and real-world policy challenges

Hosted by TEDS

TEDS stands for Transdisciplinary Econometrics & Data Science Seminar. It serves as an umbrella for thematic workshops and focused symposia that connect theory, computation, and applications.

MyJP-SIM² is part of the broader TEDS initiative, which aims to foster collaboration across econometrics, statistics, and data science.

Accommodation

Tokyo offers abundant accommodation options within the metropolitan area. Guests are welcome to make arrangements according to their preference. As an example, the following three hotels are introduced for your consideration.

KICHIJOJI TOKYU REI HOTEL

This hotel is available at Hosei University’s corporate contract rate and is located near the Hosei University Koganei Campus, the venue of the symposium. Situated in the pleasant neighborhood of Kichijoji, the property provides a tranquil atmosphere together with excellent transportation access. The short commute to the campus makes it suitable for guests who value proximity and a smoothly organized daily schedule.

Hotel website: https://www.tokyuhotels.co.jp/en/kichijoji-r/index.html

PRINCE HOTEL SHINJUKU

This hotel stands in central Shinjuku, one of Tokyo’s most dynamic districts. The area is renowned for its remarkable connectivity and a wide range of amenities, offering visitors a convenient base for exploration, meetings, and access to major terminals. It is recommended for participants who appreciate lively urban surroundings and effortless mobility.

Hotel website: https://www.princehotels.com/shinjuku/

HOTEL NEW OTANI TOKYO

Recognized as a landmark among Japan’s distinguished luxury hotels, Hotel New Otani Tokyo is celebrated for its sense of elegance and for the beautiful Japanese garden set within its spacious grounds. Despite its location in the heart of the metropolis, the environment remains serene and refined, inviting restful moments and high-level exchange. The property appeals to guests who seek comfort, scenery, and the gracious rhythm of an established premier hotel.

Hotel website: https://www.newotani.co.jp/en/tokyo/

These hotels illustrate contrasting yet complementary choices. Kichijoji Tokyu REI offers the advantage of closeness to the Koganei Campus, Prince Hotel Shinjuku assures exceptional convenience in the city center, and Hotel New Otani Tokyo provides an atmosphere of sophistication enriched by its renowned garden landscape. Guests may select freely in accordance with the style of stay they wish to enjoy in Tokyo.
