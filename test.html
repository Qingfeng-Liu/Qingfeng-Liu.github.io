ABOUT
Qingfeng Liu is a Professor at Hosei University, working in Data Science, Econometrics, and Machine Learning. His research focuses on the integration of statistical learning and economic modeling, including federated learning, causal inference, and AI-driven economic systems. He has served as Associate Editor for JASA and published in leading journals such as JBES and Econometric Reviews.
